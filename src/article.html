Understanding Mental Health

Mental health includes our emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It affects how we think, feel, and act. It also helps determine how we handle stress, relate to others, and make choices. Mental health is important at every stage of life, from childhood and adolescence through adulthood.

Mental illnesses are common and treatable. They can affect anyone, regardless of age, gender, race, or socioeconomic status. Understanding mental health is crucial for promoting well-being and preventing mental health issues.

If you or someone you know is struggling with mental health issues, it is important to seek help. There are many resources available, including hotlines, support groups, and professional counseling services.
